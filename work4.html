  • “Rapidity of Sight” 2018 Digital image

    Oil painting by the artist recreated by Google's Tensorflow Neural Network

    The original painting, done by the artists in oils of a 3D wave interference simluation, was then fed through a vision learning algorithm on a neural network hosted by Google. The algorithm continually attempted and corrected its attmept to recreate the image over the course of 100 iterations
